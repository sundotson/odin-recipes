Special Chicken Sandwich

Description

An intriguing spin on a timeless classic, with ingredients sourced from expert bakery outlets located in the faraway forest of Bornwood.

The flavours, textures and overall experience that these Bornwood-sourced ingredients add to your meal are sure to rival even that of Gordon Ramsay with even the littlest of preparation.

Ingredients

  • A rainbow brioche bun (found only in the land of Bornwood)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Fresh tomato
  • Chicken breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Renowned Bornwood eggwash
  • Fresh lettuce leaves blessed with the spirit of Bornwood
  • Pickled gherkins (optional)

Steps

  1. Place your bun on your workstation, slicing it into two halves. Butter 'em. Toast em. Spread each with mayonnaise afterwards.
  2. Take your chicken breast fillet and dunk it in a good Bornwood eggwash. Soak thoroughly.
  3. Once soaked, coat well in breadcrumbs. Toss it in the deep (or air) fryer afterwards.
  4. Once done, place on one bun half.
  5. Take great care in placing the Bornwood-spirit imbued lettuce leaf on top and take in all of its glory while doing so.
  6. Prepare and place all other ingredients on top to your liking with the other bun at the very last.
  7. Enjoy.
