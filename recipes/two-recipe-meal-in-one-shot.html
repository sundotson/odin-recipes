One Shot, Two Recipe Meal

Description

The epitome of convenience, the Bornwood Nutrition Elixir converts any and all items of nutritional value into a drinkable elixir.

Technically, by adding the item, ideally, a whole meal inside the elixir, you are adding flavour, texture and nutritional value to the elixir. This, is a breakthrough. The future of food intake.

Ingredients

  • 1x Bornwood Nutrition Elixir
  • 1x Any food item of your choosing.

Steps

  1. While in its elixir bottle, stuff any foodstuffs into it. Do not worry about overflow, the elixir will simply absorb it, and you can observe its consequent change in colour.
  2. Give it a little swirl and take a sip. Experience the taste and texture of your favourite food in a new way.
