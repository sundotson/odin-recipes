Classic Chicken Sandwich

Description

This timeless classic is as quick and easy to make as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • A brioche bun
  • Mayonnaise
  • Fresh tomato
  • Chicken breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggwash
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • Pickled gherkins

Steps

  1. Place your bun on your workstation, slicing it into two halves. Butter 'em. Toast em. Spread each with mayonnaise afterwards.
  2. Take your chicken breast fillet and dunk it in a good eggwash.
  3. Once soaked, coat well in breadcrumbs. Toss it in the deep (or air) fryer afterwards.
  4. Once done, place on one bun half.
  5. Prepare and place all other ingredients on top to your liking with the other bun at the very last.
  6. Enjoy.
